PREFACE

Born and raised in Pennsylvania, my parents taught me to enjoy the gifts of fishing, hunting, hiking, and many other forms of outdoors fun. Through grandparents and best friends' accumulation of knowledge; I developed a deep interest in all forms of alcoholic-beverage making as well. The Pennsylvania weather can turn the mood on a dime; every day is different. Through this blog, I hope to encourage all people to get outside and re-envision what the Earth has to offer.
